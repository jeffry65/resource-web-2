Datsun 240Z / Fairlady Z

The 240Z introduced the Z-car identity with a clean fastback shape, a driver-focused cabin, and an affordable sports-car feel. It became the visual starting point for the entire archive.

Overview

The original Z established the long-hood, short-deck formula that defines the archive.

Why it matters in this archive

  • Long hood and short rear deck proportions
  • Simple cockpit with analog instruments
  • Strong enthusiast following and motorsport legacy
  • The key reference point for later retro-inspired Z models
